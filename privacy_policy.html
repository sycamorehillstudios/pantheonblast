Privacy Policy — AceStorm: Sky Survivor

Effective date: July 6, 2026 · Publisher: Sycamore Hill Studios

Summary

AceStorm is an offline single-player game. We do not run our own servers, do not require an account, and do not collect, store, or sell any personal information ourselves. Your game progress is saved only on your device.

Third-party services

The app includes the following third-party SDKs, which may process data as described in their own policies:

  • Google AdMob (advertising) — the game shows only optional reward ads that you choose to watch; there are no forced or interstitial ads. AdMob may process your advertising ID, IP address, and coarse device information to serve and measure ads. In applicable regions you will be asked for consent before any ads are requested. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob data usage.
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases) — purchases are processed entirely by Google Play; we never see your payment details.
  • Google Play Games Services (optional achievements) — if you sign in, Google processes your Play Games profile per Google's Privacy Policy.

Your choices

  • All ads are opt-in reward ads — simply don't tap the ad buttons to avoid them entirely.
  • Reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalization in Android Settings → Google → Ads.
  • EEA/UK users can change their consent choice at any time via the in-app consent form.
  • Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored game data.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
